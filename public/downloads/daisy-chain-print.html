The "Interested Suitor" Test

Before you post anything, ask: Would someone actively interested in buying or selling in my market want to see this? If yes, post it. Post it again tomorrow with a different angle. If it's about you and your gratitude, rethink it.

5 Elements of Effective Prompts

  • Context: Who are you? What's your market? What's the goal?
  • Specificity: Colors, emotions, settings, actions. Think like a film director.
  • Format: Numbered list? Paragraph? Emojis? Word count? Tone?
  • Examples: Show the AI what good looks like. Paste a post you admire.
  • Constraints: "No cliches." "Under 200 words." "No exclamation marks on LinkedIn."
