The 4-Step Stacking Workflow

What goes where, in what order, with which tool. Every large language model hallucinates. Stacking catches errors before they reach your clients.

1
Generate
ChatGPT

Your enthusiastic junior associate. Talented, fast, occasionally overconfident.

First-pass content engine: property descriptions, market analyses, blog outlines, email drafts, client presentations.

Create a [TYPE OF CONTENT] about [TOPIC] for [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Include [SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS].
↓
2
Verify
Claude

Your detail-oriented quality control manager.

Fact-check the output. Identify logical inconsistencies, unsupported claims, hallucinated statistics.

Fact-check the following content. Identify any claims that cannot be verified, statistics that may be inaccurate, and statements that could be misleading. Flag each issue with a brief explanation. [PASTE CHATGPT OUTPUT]
↓
3
Fact-Check
Perplexity

Your research librarian -- the one who checks the footnotes.

Searches the live internet in real-time with citations. Verify specific statistics, market trends, and claims.

Verify the following specific claims with current sources: [LIST FLAGGED CLAIMS FROM CLAUDE]
↓
4
Correct + Format
Claude + Skill

Your graphic designer and brand manager. $20/month.

Run through your custom Brand Skill. Polished, on-brand, ready for client delivery.

Using my [BRAND NAME] skill, rewrite the following content incorporating these corrections: [LIST CORRECTIONS]. Maintain the original structure but ensure all facts are accurate and the tone matches my brand voice. [PASTE ORIGINAL CONTENT + CORRECTIONS]

When to Stack

The higher the stakes, the more AIs touch it.

Always Stack

  • Property descriptions
  • Listing presentations
  • Market analyses
  • Marketing materials
  • Client-facing content
  • Contracts

Stack Key Elements

  • Internal reports (verify stats)
  • Draft emails (confirm dates)
  • Blog posts (check claims)
  • Newsletter content

Single AI Is Fine

  • Internal communications
  • Brainstorming
  • Personal organization
  • Draft ideas for review

Application: Contract Stacking

5-Step Contract Review

  • Step 1 -- Baseline (ChatGPT): Upload contract. Identify all terms, deadlines, contingencies, obligations, non-standard clauses.
  • Step 2 -- Second Review (Claude): Same contract, separately. Compare outputs. Both flagged = immediate attention.
  • Step 3 -- Verification (Perplexity): Verify statistical claims, legal references, regulatory citations.
  • Step 4 -- Comparison (Claude): Upload both versions: "What changed between these two documents?"
  • Step 5 -- Deadlines: Extract every deadline, calculate dates, assign responsibility, generate calendar entries.

Application: Content Quality

  • Draft Phase (ChatGPT): Property details + buyer profile + tone = comprehensive first draft.
  • Critique Phase (Claude): Identify cliches, empty claims, generic sections, inconsistencies.
  • Refinement Phase (Either): Revise addressing every weakness while keeping strengths.
