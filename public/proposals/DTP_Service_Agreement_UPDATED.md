Dave T Productions LLC ("Service Provider") is pleased to present this Proposal and Service Agreement to Michael Farr and Karis Properties USA (collectively, "Client") for the design, development, and ongoing maintenance of a professional real estate website serving the Denver, Colorado metropolitan market.

## 4. PROJECT DELIVERABLES

### 4.1 Foundation Website Package (Core)

The Foundation Package includes the following minimum deliverables:

- Professionally designed responsive website using Client's existing Karis Properties brand identity, logos, and color scheme
- Up to eight (8) content pages: Home, About, Listings (or search portal), Buyers, Sellers, Communities, Blog (placeholder), Contact
- Mobile-optimized layout with fast load performance
- Contact forms with lead capture and email notification
- Social media profile links (opens in new tab — not embedded redirects)
- Google Analytics integration
- Hosting setup and domain connection assistance
- One (1) custom landing page per month (open house, event, campaign, or new listing)
- On-page SEO configuration including meta titles, descriptions, schema markup
- AI search engine visibility optimization (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google SGE)
- Mortgage calculator widget, branded to Karis Properties USA
- Closing cost estimator, configured for Colorado / RE Colorado market
- ADA / WCAG 2.1 Level AA accessibility compliance for all delivered pages

### 4.2 IDX Integration (Add-On)

If Client selects IDX integration, Service Provider will:

- Identify and recommend an appropriate IDX provider compatible with RE Colorado / SMDRA feeds
- Integrate live MLS data into the Client website, including property search, individual listing pages, saved search functionality, and lead capture
- Brand all IDX-facing pages consistently with Karis Properties USA identity
- Set up and configure Client's IDX feed with RE Colorado MLS

### 4.3 Timeline

**Foundation Package (without IDX):** Estimated delivery within three (3) to four (4) weeks of signed agreement and receipt of initial payment.

**Foundation Package with IDX Integration:** Estimated delivery within six (6) to eight (8) weeks of signed agreement, receipt of initial payment, and completion of IDX provider onboarding.

Estimated timelines are not guaranteed. Service Provider shall notify Client promptly of any anticipated delays exceeding five (5) Business Days. Timelines may be affected by Client's responsiveness in providing content, logos, and approvals, as well as IDX provider processing times.
