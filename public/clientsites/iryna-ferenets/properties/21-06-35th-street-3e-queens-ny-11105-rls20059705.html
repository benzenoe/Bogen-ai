Welcome to this beautifully refreshed two-bedroom co-op, ideally located just off vibrant Ditmars Boulevard. Filled with sunlight from its north and south exposures, the home offers a warm and inviting atmosphere with hardwood floors throughout and a thoughtfully updated kitchen featuring sleek stainless steel appliances. The flexible layout easily accommodates two bedrooms, a spacious living area, and a formal dining space, while charming details like exposed brick and newly renovated finishes add character and comfort.

Peaceful courtyard views on one side and dynamic street views on the other create a perfect balance of tranquility and neighborhood energy. Situated only a few steps up in a well-kept, pet-friendly walk-up building, the residence is moments from beloved restaurants, cozy cafés, bookstores, top-rated schools, and Astoria's lively nightlife. Transportation is effortless with the N/W trains and M60 bus nearby. Subletting is permitted after two years, offering valuable long-term flexibility.

Financing is available through an approved lender with a minimum 20% down payment. Don't miss the chance to own a bright and inviting home in one of Astoria's most desirable enclaves.
