The shape of the problem

The brand looks commercially real but not yet operator-tight. Three different free-shipping thresholds publicly visible ($100 / $149 / $150). An empty men's collection while men's pickleball is in nav. A split returns policy. A massive seasonal-drop SKU matrix without (yet) visible forecasting discipline. None of this is a brand problem — it's exactly the kind of operational surface that AI + lifecycle automation can professionalize fast.

The conversation

Discovery framework — nine sections we'll work through together

Tactical advice for the meeting itself: lead with sections 1 and 3. Most discovery calls die because the consultant front-loads stack questions before earning the right with strategic ones. Get them talking about pain in their own words first, then pivot to stack to map solutions. Save pricing/timeline for the last 5 minutes.

1Business context & strategic priorities▼
  • What's the #1 commercial goal for the next 12 months — top-line growth, margin, retention, international expansion, wholesale, or something else?
  • Where do you make most of your money today — DTC, wholesale, leagues/teams programs, or licensing collabs (K-Swiss, Simone Jardim)?
  • What's been your biggest growth lever in the last 24 months, and what's plateauing?
  • Who do you see as your top 3 competitors, and where are you losing share?
2Current stack & data▼
  • Full stack confirmation — Shopify Plus, Klaviyo, Gorgias/Zendesk, Loop Returns: what else? PIM, ERP, returns analytics?
  • Where does customer data live, and is it unified anywhere (CDP, warehouse)?
  • Do you have a data team, or is it agency / contractor-driven?
  • What's already automated vs. still manual?
3Pain points — the real ones▼
  • What three things eat the most hours of your team every week that shouldn't?
  • Where do you lose the most money to inefficiency — returns, customer service tickets, ad waste, dead inventory, content production?
  • What's the return rate by category, and what are the top 3 stated reasons? (Apparel sizing is usually the silent killer.)
  • Customer service ticket volume per week and top 5 ticket categories?
4Customer experience & merchandising▼
  • How is product discovery handled today — manual collection curation, search, recommendations? Conversion rate on PDP vs. search vs. collection pages?
  • Any size recommendation tool in place? What's the size-related return %?
  • How personalized is the homepage / email / SMS today, and what tools drive it?
  • How do you handle the multi-sport buyer (tennis + pickleball + golf overlap)?
5Content & marketing▼
  • Who writes product descriptions, email copy, social captions today? How long does a new collection launch take from sample to live?
  • How many SKUs launch per quarter, and what's the bottleneck — photography, copy, merchandising?
  • Are you using AI-generated imagery, video, or copy anywhere yet? What's worked, what hasn't?
  • Paid acquisition: CAC trend, blended ROAS, channel mix?
6B2B / wholesale / community▼
  • How are leagues, teams, and brand ambassadors managed operationally — portal, email, spreadsheets?
  • Bulk orders, custom team colors — how much of that is manual?
  • Wholesale order volume relative to DTC?
7Returns & operations▼
  • Loop Returns is in — what's the return rate, and how much of returns processing is automated end-to-end?
  • Inventory planning: how do you forecast demand for new collections vs. core/essentials?
  • Any chronic dead-stock problem on prints/seasonal vs. core colors?
8Decision-making & engagement fit▼
  • Who owns the AI/automation conversation internally — CEO, COO, CMO, CTO/Head of Digital?
  • Have you evaluated AI vendors or consultants before? What worked or didn't?
  • Budget posture for the next 12 months — exploratory pilot, defined initiative, or open?
  • What does success look like 90 days after we start working together?
  • Decision timeline and process — who else needs to be in the room?
9Close-the-meeting questions▼
  • If we could solve only one problem for you in Q1, what would it be?
  • What would make this engagement a "hell yes" for you?
  • What would make it a "no"?
