Bogen.ai vs Lofty

Understanding the difference between software platforms and strategic consulting

The question comes up often: "What's the difference between Bogen.ai and Lofty?" On the surface, both help real estate professionals capture and convert inbound leads. But the models are fundamentally different.

Lofty is software. You buy the platform, they train you on the features, and you run it. Bogen.ai is consulting. We build a custom system tailored to your business, integrate AI phone agents, create your content, and manage the operation with you. One is a product. The other is a partnership.

The Models

Two Different Approaches

Lofty
All-in-One CRM Platform

Lofty is a comprehensive real estate CRM that combines lead capture, automation, marketing tools, and transaction management into a single platform. You pay for the software, attend their training, and operate it yourself.

  • AI-powered phone system (Lofty AI Assistant)
  • Lead capture tools (website, landing pages, social ads)
  • Email & SMS automation
  • Transaction management
  • Team management & reporting
  • Mobile app for agents
  • Integration marketplace (Zillow, Realtor.com, etc.)
Bogen.ai
Consulting & Implementation Service

Bogen.ai builds a custom Inbound Conversion System tailored to your business. We audit your current process, design the workflows, integrate AI phone agents, create your content, build your automations, and manage the system with you ongoing.

  • 90-minute business assessment & gap analysis
  • Custom AI phone agent setup (My AI Front Desk or Bland AI)
  • Branded landing pages, lead magnets, and QR codes
  • CRM integration (Follow Up Boss, kvCORE, etc.)
  • Done-for-you email sequences & nurture campaigns
  • AI-generated content & market reports
  • Weekly strategy calls & performance coaching
  • Ongoing optimization & system management
Key Difference: Lofty gives you the tools and expects you to run them. Bogen.ai builds the system for you, creates the content, and manages the operation with you. If you want software you control, choose Lofty. If you want a partner who builds and runs the system with you, choose Bogen.ai.

Decision Framework

Which One Is Right for You?

Choose Lofty If...
  • You want an all-in-one software platform you control
  • You have time to learn the system and manage it daily
  • You need transaction management and team tools
  • You prefer lower monthly costs and DIY implementation
  • You're comfortable building your own content and automations
Choose Bogen.ai If...
  • You want a partner who builds and runs the system with you
  • You don't have time to learn and manage another platform
  • You want custom AI phone agents tailored to your business
  • You need done-for-you content creation and optimization
  • You value strategic coaching and ongoing system management
Bottom Line: Lofty and Bogen.ai aren't really competitors. They serve different needs. If you're a software-savvy team that wants control and doesn't mind managing the operation, Lofty is a strong platform. If you're looking for a consulting partner to design, build, and optimize a custom system for you—and you value the strategic relationship—Bogen.ai is built for that. The right choice depends on what you need and how you work.
