The Process

7 Steps to Conversion

Step 1

CAPTURE

Build the inbound engine with multiple branded entry points.

  • AI phone agent answers every call (no voicemail)
  • Branded landing pages & lead magnets
  • QR code systems
  • Auto-creates lead in CRM with source tracking
Step 2

QUALIFY

Score and segment immediately within 5 minutes of capture.

  • AI asks qualifying questions live on call
  • Hot/Warm/Nurture segmentation
  • Automatic CRM tagging
  • Smart routing to follow-up sequences
Step 3

RESPOND

Personalized first touch that builds trust immediately.

  • AI phone agent IS the first response (0.5s)
  • Hot leads trigger instant SMS alerts
  • AI-drafted personalized responses
  • Follow-up text with resource links mid-call
Step 4

NURTURE

Stay top-of-mind with consistent, valuable content.

  • 30/60/90-day drip sequences
  • AI-generated market intelligence
  • Automated email with dynamic content
  • Premium: Outbound AI calls to non-responders
Step 5

CONVERT

Move qualified leads into high-value strategy sessions.

  • AI books sessions on Google Calendar
  • Automated pre-session briefing packets
  • AI-generated CMAs & market reports
  • Smart scheduling with reminders
Step 6

CLOSE

Execute with flawless transaction management.

  • 7/3/1-day alert system for critical dates
  • AI-powered timeline management
  • Automated status updates
  • Predictive issue flagging
Step 7

MULTIPLY

Turn every closed client into a referral source.

  • AI post-close calls at 30/60/90 days
  • Automated review request sequences
  • Non-negotiable referral ask at Day 30
  • Annual touchpoint calendar
RULE: EVERY PIECE OF CONTENT MUST HAVE A CAPTURE MECHANISM. NO DEAD ENDS.
