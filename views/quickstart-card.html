Day 1 30 min Both
Create Your AI Accounts
Create ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) and Claude Pro ($20/mo) accounts. Run one conversation with each -- ask about your neighborhood market. Get familiar with how each responds differently.
Reference: Chapter 2 (Your AI Toolkit)
Day 2 45 min Claude
Generate Your First AI-Powered CMA
Export MLS data for your farm area -- sold and active listings as CSV. Generate your first AI-powered CMA using the master prompt in Chapter 3. Print it. You now have a deliverable.
Reference: Chapter 3 (The CMA Machine)
Day 3 20 min ChatGPT
Create and Post Your First AI Content
Create one piece of social media content using the Interested Suitor framework: "Would someone actively looking to buy or sell in my market want to see this?" Post it today. Not tomorrow.
Reference: Chapter 4 (Content Creation)
Day 4 30 min Claude
Build Your First Claude Skill
Create a reusable instruction set for your brand voice -- your colors, tone, audience, territory. Every piece of content from here on will be on-brand automatically. Build once, invoke forever.
Reference: Chapter 5 (Claude Skills)
Day 5 30 min Claude
Set Up the Risk Radar
Upload your state's standard contract to a Claude Project. Run your first Risk Radar comparison on a recent contract. Compare what AI catches versus what you caught manually.
Reference: Chapter 11 (Risk Radar)
Day 6 20 min ChatGPT
Generate Your First Client Whisperer Update
Create a weekly update for an active transaction using the Client Whisperer prompt. Personalized, professional, and ready to send. Your clients will notice the difference immediately.
Reference: Chapter 8 (Client Communication)
Day 7 30 min Both
Review, Adjust, Commit
Review all outputs from the week. Adjust prompts that didn't produce great results. Establish your daily AI habit -- block 30 minutes on your calendar, every day, starting now.
Reference: Chapter 16 (Implementation Plan)
