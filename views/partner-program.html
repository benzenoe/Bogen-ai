Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I earn commissions for each referral?

As long as the client remains active. If they stay for 2 years, you earn for 2 years. If they stay for 10 years, you earn for 10 years.

What if my referral cancels after a few months?

You earn commissions only while they're active. If they cancel, commissions stop. But most clients stay long-term because the AI automation delivers real ROI.

How do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly via Xero. You'll receive an invoice and payment within 5 business days of month-end.

Do I need to be a salesperson?

No. You just make introductions. We handle all proposals, technical questions, contracts, and delivery. You stay focused on relationships.

Can I refer clients in any industry?

Yes! We serve real estate, legal, medical, home services, e-commerce, consulting, and more. If they're a business with repetitive tasks and customers, we can help.

Is there a cost to join?

No. Partner program is 100% free. No fees, no monthly charges, no hidden costs.

What support do I get as a partner?

You get a dedicated partner dashboard, marketing materials, email/social templates, and access to our partner support team whenever you need help.
