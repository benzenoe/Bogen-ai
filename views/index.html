Meet Edmund Bogen

From luxury real estate leader to AI pioneer, Edmund built his career on innovation and results. As a top-producing agent with Douglas Elliman in South Florida, he saw firsthand how AI could transform business operations.

After coaching 200+ agents through Mastermind (REIGNation) and implementing AI across his real estate empire, Edmund founded bogen.ai to democratize enterprise-grade AI automation for growing businesses everywhere.

"AI should work for business owners, not replace human relationships. That's the principle behind every service we build."

Powered by Eytan Benzeno

Behind every bogen.ai service is enterprise-grade infrastructure built by AI engineer Eytan Benzeno. With deep expertise in machine learning, LLM integration, and workflow automation, Eytan transforms complex AI capabilities into simple, reliable business tools.

Using Claude (Anthropic) as our primary AI engine, Eytan custom-integrates with your existing systems—CRM, email, calendars, databases—creating automation that feels invisible because it just works.
