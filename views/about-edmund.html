From Luxury Real Estate to AI Innovation

Edmund Bogen

Founder & CEO, Bogen.ai

From NYC to South Florida Success

Edmund's career began in New York City's fast-paced PR world at First Degree, where he learned the art of storytelling and relationship-building. But real estate was calling, and he answered—transforming from a newcomer into a record-breaking agent.

At Douglas Elliman, Edmund established himself as a leader in South Florida's luxury market, specializing in high-end properties from Palm Beach to Miami. His client-first approach and market expertise built a thriving business based on trust, results, and referrals.

Building REIGNation: Mastermind

Success in real estate led to a new calling: helping other agents achieve similar results. Edmund founded Mastermind (REIGNation), an elite coaching program that has guided 200+ agents to new levels of production and profitability.

Through REIGNation, Edmund saw a pattern: agents struggled with the same operational challenges—follow-up, lead management, client communication, and content creation. They needed leverage, not more hours in the day.

Early AI Adopter to Industry Pioneer

When AI tools like Claude emerged, Edmund didn't just experiment—he fully integrated them into his business. Email automation, lead qualification, social media content, contract review, client follow-up. Every repetitive task became an opportunity for AI leverage.

The results were immediate: more deals closed with less stress. More time for high-value activities like client meetings and strategy. More revenue with the same-sized team.

But Edmund realized something bigger: This technology shouldn't be exclusive to tech-savvy early adopters.

Democratizing Enterprise AI

That realization became bogen.ai. Partnering with AI engineer Eytan Benzeno, Edmund set out to make enterprise-grade AI automation accessible to small and mid-sized businesses across every industry.

Real estate. Legal. Medical. Home services. E-commerce. Any business with repetitive tasks, customer communication, and growth goals could benefit from the same AI leverage that transformed Edmund's own operations.

Today, bogen.ai offers 50+ AI services—from 24/7 customer assistants to automated proposal generation to social media content creation—all delivered with white-glove service and transparent pricing.

Edmund's Philosophy

"AI should work for business owners, not replace human relationships. The goal isn't to remove people from the equation—it's to free them up for the high-value work only humans can do: strategy, creativity, relationship-building, and growth."

Today

Edmund splits his time between South Florida luxury real estate, coaching elite agents through REIGNation, and scaling bogen.ai to serve growing businesses nationwide.

When he's not building AI systems or closing deals, you'll find him at St. Andrews Country Club or enjoying the Miami lifestyle with his dog, Coco.
