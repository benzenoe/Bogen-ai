Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 9, 2025

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Dave T Productions, LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), a Florida limited liability company, operates Bogen.ai (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site or engage with our mastermind groups, coaching services, and AI consulting offerings.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business information when you register for services
  • Payment and billing information for mastermind memberships, coaching sessions, and consulting engagements
  • Communication preferences and correspondence with our team
  • Professional background, real estate credentials, and business goals shared during coaching sessions

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Referral sources and search terms used to find our Site

Video and Audio Content:

  • Video and audio recordings of mastermind sessions, coaching calls, and training events
  • Screen recordings and presentations shared during sessions
  • Photographs taken at in-person mastermind events and speaking engagements

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide mastermind group access, coaching services, and AI consulting
  • Process payments and manage your membership or service agreements
  • Send educational content, training materials, and program updates
  • Record, produce, and distribute video content featuring mastermind sessions and training events for educational and promotional purposes
  • Improve our services and develop new offerings
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send marketing communications about Edmund's Mastermind and related services (with opt-out option)
  • Analyze Site usage to enhance user experience

4. Video Recording and Content Distribution

By participating in our mastermind groups, coaching sessions, or events, you acknowledge and consent to the following:

  • Sessions may be recorded via video and/or audio for quality assurance, training, and content distribution
  • Recorded content may be edited, reproduced, and distributed through:
    • Private member portals and learning management systems
    • Social media platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok)
    • Marketing materials, promotional videos, and testimonials
    • Educational courses and training programs
    • Speaking engagement materials and presentations
  • Your name, likeness, voice, and statements may appear in these recordings
  • We retain ownership rights to all recorded content
  • You waive any right to royalties or compensation for the use of your appearance in these materials

Opt-Out Options:

  • If you do not wish to appear in distributed content, you may request to have your camera off during virtual sessions
  • For in-person events, notify us in writing at least 48 hours in advance if you do not consent to being recorded
  • We will make reasonable efforts to exclude you from promotional materials upon request, though we cannot guarantee removal from all existing content

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, email marketing platforms, video hosting services (Zoom, Vimeo, YouTube), and CRM systems that help us operate our business
  • Business Partners: Eytan Benzeno and other collaborators involved in delivering AI consulting services through Bogen.ai
  • Real Estate Affiliates: Douglas Elliman Real Estate and related business entities when relevant to coaching or consulting services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or transfer of our business assets

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-compliant providers
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security assessments and updates

However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request removal from future video recordings and promotional materials
  • Object to certain processing of your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at edmund@bogenhomes.com or 561-235-7575.

8. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance Site functionality and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings, though this may limit certain Site features.

9. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites (social media platforms, partner sites, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business professionals and individuals 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

11. International Users

Our services are based in Florida, USA. If you access our Site from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where data protection laws may differ from your jurisdiction.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
