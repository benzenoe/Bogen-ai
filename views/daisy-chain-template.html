~2 min

Paste your transcript into Claude (or ChatGPT) with the Mega-Prompt below. In 90 seconds you get: a complete summary, key takeaways, and raw material for everything that follows.

Analyze the provided source material carefully. First, write a comprehensive summary along with key takeaways and actionable items. All content must be actionable. Step 1: Extract 3-5 key takeaways that are clear, impactful, and applicable by professionals aiming to grow their businesses. Step 2: Generate the following content: YOUTUBE DESCRIPTION (SEO-optimized, under 5,000 characters): - Include emojis, keywords, hooks, and timestamps - Include [YOUR NAME] in the title - Add relevant hashtags for discoverability FACEBOOK (5 Posts): - Concise, engaging, actionable tips with emojis - Include questions to stimulate interaction - Vary topics across the 5 posts; add hashtags INSTAGRAM (3 Posts): - 1 carousel post with descriptions for 3-5 images - 2 single-image/video posts with compelling insights - Include relevant emojis and hashtags LINKEDIN (3 Posts): - Professional tone, valuable insights - Use emojis sparingly; highlight real-world applications - Link one post to the blog AI AVATAR SCRIPTS (3 Scripts, 30-60 seconds each): - Start with 'This is A.I. 3
YouTube Description
~3 min

The Mega-Prompt generates an SEO-optimized description with hooks, timestamps, keywords, and hashtags -- under 5,000 characters. This would take 30-45 minutes manually.

  • Copy the description from the AI output
  • Add your video link and any specific timestamps
  • Upload to YouTube along with your video content

YouTube is a Search Engine

Content you post today generates views and leads for years. Your description is an SEO asset. Titles with specificity, numbers, and curiosity gaps drive clicks.

4
Social Media Content
~10 min

Five Facebook posts, three Instagram posts (one carousel, two single-image), three LinkedIn articles. Each platform-optimized -- not copy-pasted.

  • Facebook: End with questions. "What neighborhood are YOU trying to own?" drives engagement.
  • Instagram: Carousels outperform single images. Five slides with one clear, bold statement each.
  • LinkedIn: Thought leader tone. Lead with the insight, tell a brief story, invite discussion.

Bonus: The "Interested Suitor" Test

Every piece of content should pass this test before you post it.

+
Quality Check for Every Post

Ask yourself: Would someone actively interested in buying or selling in my market want to see this?

  • Fails the test: "Just sold! So grateful for another amazing closing with the best clients ever!" -- that's about you. Nobody searching for homes cares about your gratitude.
  • Passes the test: "This 4-bedroom just closed at $87 per square foot above the community average. Here's what that tells us about where pricing is headed." -- same sale, completely different value to the reader.

If the content serves your audience, frequency is a feature. If it's generic "just listed/just sold" posts, one a week feels like spam.

Bonus: 5 Elements of Effective Prompts

AI is only as good as the instructions you give it.

+
The Prompt Quality Framework
  • Context: Who are you? What's your market? What's the goal?
  • Specificity: Colors, lighting, character descriptions, emotions, settings. Think like a film director.
  • Format: Numbered list? Paragraph? Include emojis? Word count? Tone?
  • Examples: Show the AI what good looks like. Paste in a post you admire.
  • Constraints: "Don't use real estate cliches." "Keep it under 200 words." "No exclamation marks in the LinkedIn version."
